This section of the histo.fyi website contains information and structure predictions of Classical MHC Class I alleles.
Within this resource you can find information on locations of polymorphisms, peptide binding motifs where known, lists of structures and statistics on the quality of structure predictions.
It is a resource which will grow over time, with more data being added for non-Human Class I and Class II alleles in due course.
